## Product Direction

Build a PC-first dashboard for a personal schedule management AI agent. Mobile will be handled separately later.

## Layout

- Top command bar: product title, global search, date selector, day/week/month switch, alert count, profile.
- Left panel: compact navigation, mini month calendar, schedule summary.
- Center panel: main calendar workspace with day/week/month views and editable event list.
- Right panel: AI schedule input, manual registration, important alerts, priority recommendations.

## Visual Style

- Modern B2B productivity dashboard.
- White main background, light gray panels, deep navy primary text.
- Compact information density for desktop use.
- Avoid decorative visuals and marketing layout.
